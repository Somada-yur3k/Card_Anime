DEMONS SLAYER

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed by demons, and his sister Nezuko is transformed into one. Together, they embark on a journey to find a cure for Nezuko and defeat the demon responsible for their tragedy, all while encountering powerful demons and honing their skills.

BLACK CLOVER

Black Clover is about Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where everyone has magical abilities. Despite this, Asta dreams of becoming the Wizard King, the strongest mage in the kingdom. With hard work, a mysterious anti-magic power, and the help of his friends, he strives to achieve his goal and protect the Clover Kingdom from various threats.

SOLO LEVELING

Solo Leveling centers around Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in a world where humans fight monsters and dungeons to survive.
